The data was taken from the "SPARK Challenge : SPAcecraft Recognition leveraging Knowledge of Space Environment" stream-1:
https://cvi2.uni.lu/spark2022/
https://uniluxembourg-my.sharepoint.com/personal/arunkumar_rathinam_uni_lu/_layouts/15/onedrive.aspx?id=%2Fpersonal%2Farunkumar%5Frathinam%5Funi%5Flu%2FDocuments%2FShare%2FSPARK%2D2022%2FStream%2D1&ga=1


It contains images with 11 categories (10 spacecrafts and 1 debris) in a space environment.

The data is divided in:
- Training dataset:   66,000 images (6,000 images per category) ------ 113  GB
- Test dataset:       22,000 images (2,000 images per category) ------ 37.6 GB
- Validation dataset: 22,000 images (2,000 images per category) ------ 37.6 GB
